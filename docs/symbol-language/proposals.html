A symbol language for DeepResearch.Se/rver

DeepResearch.Se/cure already speaks in symbols: the ghost (anonymity) holding pink umbrellas (shelter), the first-visit umbrella intro, and an umbrella landing for every completed task. DeepResearch.Se/rver needs its own language — positive, and it should tell you something true about the tier: memory, stewardship, reach, lift.

Four candidates below, each animated in the blue tier's palette. Every card has a ✓ complete a task button — press it a few times to see the per-task landing event (the umbrella-landing counterpart). Pick one, or a combination.

DECIDED (owner, 2026-07-15): the balloons. And refined: the balloon itself is the symbol — one little balloon following you around as the tier's guide, just as the ghost does on the secure side, hovering among the clouds and swishing by clouds in all of its transitions. The preview below shows the guide as shipped (public/js/balloon.js); candidates B–D stay for the record.

THE PICK · AS SHIPPED

The balloon guide — hovering among the clouds

0 tasks
Where it lives
A small corner of the app above the composer — the ghost's counterpart, always with you, always among its clouds.
Per completed task
The burner flares gold, the balloon climbs a notch, a pennant unfurls under the basket — and clouds streak downward past it: the relative motion of the climb.
All other transitions
Appearing at boot and resetting for a new chat, clouds swish past it sideways. Every move the guide makes goes through clouds.
The ghost holds umbrellas — sheltered. The balloon hovers among clouds — carried: the server does the lifting.
CANDIDATE A · ✓ CHOSEN 2026-07-15

The Lift — hot-air balloons

0 tasks
Signifies
The server carries the load. Balloons are the umbrellas' geometric sibling — the same gored canopy, but powered and rising instead of sheltering.
Intro
The logo vortex untwists and inflates into balloon envelopes in flag gold-and-blue; baskets and rigging fade in.
Per task
The burner flares gold, the balloon rises a notch, and a pennant unfurls under the basket — altitude is visible progress.
Mascot
The balloonist/pilot (counterpart of the ghost: the one doing the carrying).
The umbrella says “sheltered — nothing leaves.” The balloon answers: “carried — the server does the lifting.”

The Keeper — lighthouse over a harbor

0 tasks
Signifies
Stewardship. The signed-in tier is always on, watching over your work and guiding it safely home.
Intro
The vortex spins itself into the lamp room; its twist unwraps into the tower's spiral stripe; the first beam sweeps on.
Per task

Mascot
The lighthouse keeper.
The umbrella says “sheltered.” The lighthouse answers: “watched over — and remembered: the lamps stay lit.”

The Star Chart — a growing constellation

0 tasks
Signifies
Memory. Every completed task is a fixed point of knowledge; the session literally draws your research map — and the sky keeps it.
Intro
The vortex is a wheel of golden sparks; it flings them outward and they settle as the night sky (logo gold on flag blue — the palette is already the Swedish flag at night).
Per task
A star ignites and a golden line connects it to the last one — the constellation grows with your work.
Mascot
The astronomer — or no figure at all: the sky itself is the keeper.
The umbrella says “sheltered.” The constellation answers: “remembered — your work joins a picture that persists.”
