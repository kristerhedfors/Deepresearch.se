How to read this

Every commit is tagged with the feature sets its message mentions — zero, one, or several. Each colored series is one subject; its height at a point in time is how much of that period's work touched it. Lines compares subjects head-to-head; Stream shows the share of total focus (a multi-subject commit is split across its tags). Weigh by raw commits, or by lines changed as a rough proxy for how much work — and how many tokens — a theme absorbed. It's a keyword heuristic over commit subjects, so read it as the shape of attention, not an exact ledger.
