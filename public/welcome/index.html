First time here? What this does — and doesn't

It does

  • Research your question with web searches it plans itself, and answer with numbered citations — within a time budget you set.
  • Signed in — deepresearch.se/rver — add documents, photos, projects and cloud sync on EU-hosted models. Sign in with the account button (top right); accounts are invite-only and await approval.
  • Offer a no-account tier — deepresearch.se/cure — that runs entirely in your browser on your own API key.

It doesn't

  • No ads, no trackers, nothing sold — an open-source research project, not a commercial product.
  • No open signup: new accounts wait for the operator's approval.
  • In the se/cure tier it doesn't even see your chats — the server is not in the data path at all, by construction.
