A showcase, not a service

Everything here runs from one public repository. The site is an exploration: where can the lines be drawn between privacy and security, between open and closed — and can a working product make those lines visible instead of just claiming them?

Every major feature leaves behind a skill — a distilled playbook in the repository — so you can take one and build the same capability your own way, in your own repo. The aim is to be directly useful, verifiable, and new, all at once.

First time here?

This site does deep research: it plans web searches, reads the results, and answers with numbered citations. There are two ways in.

deepresearch.se/cure

  • No account. Runs in your browser: bring an OpenAI or Berget API key, or a local server such as Ollama.
  • Chats, keys and projects stay on your device. This site's server never receives them.
  • Enter through the glowing ghost, top right.

deepresearch.se/rver

  • The hosted service: live web search within a time budget you set, documents and photos, projects, cloud sync. EU-hosted models by default.
  • No ads, no trackers, nothing sold. Costs are covered by per-account quotas.
  • Sign in with the account button, top right. Accounts are invite-only and wait for approval.

This is an open showcase, not a service. Read the build story — every prompt, verbatim — or see about this project, the documentation, and the source code.
